Money Laundering Detection App

Money laundering, the process of disguising the origins of illegally obtained money, is a global menace that undermines the integrity of financial systems and economies worldwide. Nigeria, Africa's most populous country and one of its largest economies, grapples with significant challenges posed by money laundering. Despite regulatory efforts and international cooperation, the country continues to battle the complex web of illicit financial activities that threaten its stability and development. This article delves into the intricate dynamics of money laundering in Nigeria, exploring its root causes, prevalent methods, regulatory responses, and the imperative for sustained efforts to combat this pervasive crime.

This App uses a Deep Learning model that is trained on previus credit card transactions to predict the probability of a transaction been fraudulent based on the details of the transaction so that fraud can be prevented.
